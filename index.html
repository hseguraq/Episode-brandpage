About Us

Since 2006, Episode had one goal in mind: to make the industry's best speaker. In crafting their first speakers, they used premium materials to build innovative designs with custom integrators in mind. The result? Speakers that sound and function like nothing else on the market. Episode has expanded to include architectural, commercial and outdoor audio solutions.
